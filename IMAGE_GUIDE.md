## Tips for Finding Images
- **Breakfast**: Search "Dubai Creek restaurant breakfast" or "Mazmi Dubai"
- **Adventure**: Search "Chaos Kart Dubai" or "AR go-kart racing Dubai"
- **Dinner**: Search "Melanzane Dubai" or "Italian fine dining Dubai"
- **Movie**: Search "Now You See Me 2013 movie poster" and "cozy movie night setup"
- Use free stock photo sites: **Unsplash**, **Pexels**, or **Pixabay**
- For movie images, check official movie databases: **IMDb**, **The Movie Database (TMDb)**
- Ensure images have proper licensing for web use
- Resize large images to reduce file size (under 5MB each)
