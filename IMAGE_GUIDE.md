# Image Guide for Birthday Website

## Breakfast Experience Images
You'll need to add 4 images for the Mazmi Dubai breakfast experience. Place these images in the `images/` folder:

### 1. breakfast-main.jpg (Hero Image)
- **Purpose**: Main hero image for the breakfast page
- **Dimensions**: Landscape, minimum 1920x400px recommended
- **Content**: Elegant Dubai Creek waterfront view, preferably showing:
  - Dubai Creek water view
  - Morning/golden hour lighting
  - Restaurant ambiance or outdoor seating
  - Arabian/Middle Eastern architecture
- **Style**: Warm, inviting, romantic

### 2. breakfast-view.jpg (Gallery Image 1)
- **Purpose**: Showcase the creek/water view
- **Dimensions**: Square or landscape, minimum 600x600px
- **Content**:
  - Dubai Creek panoramic view
  - Traditional dhow boats (optional)
  - Waterfront scenery
  - Morning light on water
- **Style**: Serene, peaceful

### 3. breakfast-food.jpg (Gallery Image 2)
- **Purpose**: Show the gourmet breakfast spread
- **Dimensions**: Square or landscape, minimum 600x600px
- **Content**:
  - Beautifully plated breakfast dishes
  - International or Middle Eastern breakfast items
  - Fresh pastries, fruits, eggs, etc.
  - Elegant table setting
- **Style**: Appetizing, colorful, fresh

### 4. breakfast-ambiance.jpg (Gallery Image 3)
- **Purpose**: Capture the restaurant atmosphere
- **Dimensions**: Square or landscape, minimum 600x600px
- **Content**:
  - Restaurant interior or outdoor seating
  - Elegant table settings
  - Cozy, romantic atmosphere
  - Soft lighting, decorative elements
- **Style**: Intimate, romantic, luxurious

## Tips for Finding Images
- Search for "Dubai Creek restaurant breakfast" on free stock photo sites
- Look for "waterfront dining Dubai" or "Arabian breakfast"
- Check Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay for royalty-free images
- Ensure images have proper licensing for web use
- Resize large images to reduce file size (under 5MB each)
