# Media Files

## Required Files

Please add your media files to these folders:

### Videos Folder (`/videos`)
1. **birthday-video.mp4** - The birthday celebration video that plays after password entry
2. **finale-video.mp4** - Your thank you message video at the end

### Audio Folder (`/audio`)
1. **romantic-song.mp3** - The background music that plays throughout the experience

## Tips for Media Files

### Video Files
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) is recommended for best compatibility
- **Resolution**: 1080p or 720p works great
- **Length**: Keep each video 1-3 minutes for best experience
- **Audio**: Videos can have their own audio, which will play alongside the background music

### Audio File
- **Format**: MP3 is recommended
- **Quality**: 128-320 kbps
- **Length**: Choose a song that's meaningful to both of you
- **Note**: The song will loop automatically throughout the experience

## How to Add Files

1. Place your video files in the `videos/` folder
2. Place your audio file in the `audio/` folder
3. Make sure the file names match exactly:
   - `birthday-video.mp4`
   - `finale-video.mp4`
   - `romantic-song.mp3`

## Converting Videos

If your videos are in a different format, you can convert them using:

### Online Tools
- CloudConvert.com
- Online-Convert.com

### Desktop Tools (Free)
- **macOS**: QuickTime Player (File > Export As)
- **HandBrake**: Free, cross-platform video converter
- **VLC Media Player**: Media > Convert/Save
