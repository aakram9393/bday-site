## Tips for Great Hint Photos

### Option 1: Subtle Hints
- Take a photo of the general area without showing the exact hiding spot
- Example: A photo of the living room with the couch visible
- This makes it more of a fun treasure hunt!

### Option 2: Clear Arrows
- Take a photo and add arrows pointing to the location
- Use your phone's photo editor or apps like:
  - **Markup** (iPhone built-in)
  - **Snapseed** (free app)
  - **Canva** (free online editor)

### Option 3: Zoomed Out View
- Show the room from a distance
- Let her figure out where to look

### Option 4: Mystery Angle
- Take photos from interesting angles
- Make it a fun guessing game

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Recommended 1200x800px or similar (will auto-resize)
- **Orientation**: Landscape (horizontal) works best
- **File names**: Must be exactly as listed above

## How to Add Images

### Method 1: Drag and Drop (After downloading photos)
1. Take photos on your phone
2. AirDrop/email them to your computer
3. Rename them to match the names above
4. Drag them into this `images/` folder

### Method 2: Direct from iPhone
1. Connect iPhone to Mac
2. Open **Photos** app
3. Select the photos you want
4. Export to this folder
5. Rename them accordingly

### Method 3: Upload via GitHub (if already deployed)
1. Go to your repository on GitHub
2. Navigate to `images/` folder
3. Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"
4. Drag and drop your renamed images
5. Commit the changes
